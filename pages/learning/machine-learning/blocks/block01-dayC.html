NumPy & Pandas Foundations

Models don't understand reality; they only understand numbers in arrays. Mastery starts with the translators: NumPy and Pandas.

1 NumPy: The Math Engine

NumPy is the foundation of every major ML library (Scikit-learn, PyTorch, TensorFlow). It enables Linear Algebra at scale.

Vectorization

Instead of slow Python loops, NumPy applies operations to entire arrays at once. It's faster, cleaner, and mandatory for ML.

Shapes & Dims

A model expects specific structures: (n_samples, n_features). If your shapes don't match, your model can't learn.

2 Pandas: Thinking in Tables

Pandas is designed for messy, real-world data. It lets you inspect, clean, and filter datasets before they ever touch a model.

⚙️ The Translation Rule

Machine learning models learn from numbers, not reality. NumPy and Pandas are your translators. If you get the translation wrong, the model is meaningless.

Beginner Warning: Never feed a string (like "Toronto") directly into a model. Every piece of information must become a float or integer first.
